Established in the 1950’s, Heathergems is a unique and imaginative range of Scottish jewellery and giftware, made in Pitlochry, Scotland from natural heather stems. They are the only manufacturers of this unique Scottish product anywhere in the world. Our fantastic range from Heathergems includes pendants, bangles and bracelets, brooches, earrings, rings and gift accessories. We also stock their new collection of items including photograph frames and golf markers.

The Sgian Dubh Co.

The Sgian Dubh Company has been manufacturing sgian dubhs in Scotland for over 30 years, gradually developing their range over time. They now have one of the largest – and arguably, best – sgian dubh ranges in the world. Our extensive collection from The Sgian Dubh Company includes dress and daywear sgian dubhs, pewter kilt pins, stylish chrome cufflinks, belt buckles, brooches, tankards, leather hip flasks, various quaich styles and sizes, as well as pendants, jacket buttons and other accessories.
